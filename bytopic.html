Zhize Li (李志泽)

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student at the Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University. [Google Scholar]. My Advisor is Prof. Jian Li.

I was a visiting scholar at Computer Science Department of Duke University (Hosted by Prof. Rong Ge) and a visiting scholar at Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISyE) of Georgia Institute of Technology (Hosted by Prof. Guanghui (George) Lan).

My research interests lie in theoretical computer science and machine learning, in particular (non-)convex optimization algorithms, machine learning, algorithms and data structures.
